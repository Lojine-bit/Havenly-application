Mock campaign pitch
Campaign concept — Q3 2025

"Your feed found it first."

Havenly's real product isn't makeup — it's the feeling of being the person in your friend group who always knows what's trending before anyone else. This campaign makes that identity the hero.

The insight

Trend-spotting is an identity, not a purchase reason

Havenly customers don't just want Rhode. They want to be the person who had Rhode before it sold out in Egypt. The campaign speaks to that identity — not the product.

Target audience

Egyptian women, 18–32, IG-native, beauty-obsessed

They follow global beauty accounts. They screenshot Sabrina Carpenter's gloss. They share "found it" moments. They respond to urgency, curation, and community.

Campaign duration

6-week rolling drop series

Each "drop" is treated as a cultural event, not a restock notification. Tied to global viral moments and teased 72 hours in advance across IG and email.

Instagram content series

3 content pillars

Each pillar has its own look, cadence, and purpose — together they build hype, community, and conversion.

Pillar 01 — Drop Alerts
"Your 72-hour warning. Rhode Peptide Lip Tint drops this Friday. Egypt-exclusive. Set your reminder." ⏱
Pillar 02 — The Edit
"5 products your feed has been obsessing over. We have all of them. Here's how to wear them together." ✦
Pillar 03 — Havenly Favourites
"We built the kit so you don't have to. Our most-loved products, curated into one drop." 🤍
Klaviyo email drop sequence

5-email drop cycle

Built around each product drop. Triggered by browse behaviour and purchase history segmentation.

Day −3

The tease

"Something's coming to Egypt first. We're not saying what yet." — Subject line with 0 details. Open rate play on curiosity.

Day −1

The reveal

Product name, one hero image, one sentence. Link to early-access sign-up for subscribers only. Creates list-building urgency.

Day 0

Drop day

"It's here. And it won't be for long." — Stock scarcity framing, one CTA, mobile-first layout. No distractions.

Day +3

The pair

Sent to purchasers only. "You got it. Here's what people are wearing it with." — Cross-sell into the "Havenly Favourites" kit.

Day +14

The loyalty nudge

"You were one of the first to get it in Egypt." — Early-adopter identity reinforcement. Primes for next drop with sneak peek.
